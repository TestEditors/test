In passing the W. side of the Pyramid the visitor has an opportunity of observing, that, although several triangular blocks of granite are strewn around, which perhaps once formed the lower
portion of the external incrustation of the Pyramid, the upper
quarter of the Pyramid is covered with a hard kind of conglomerate composed of limestone, broken bricks, and plaster.
