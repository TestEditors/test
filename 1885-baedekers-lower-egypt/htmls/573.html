wish to shoot the mountain-goat, which abounds here, may apply to the intendant of the monastery, who will provide them with a suitable guide.

event of the promulgation of the law, is in connection with the flight of

brook Kishon (L Kings, xviii. ur, xix. b). At an early part of the Christian
period a number of anchorites settled here amid the springs of these rocky
