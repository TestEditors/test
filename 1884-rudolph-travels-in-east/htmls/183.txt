At sunrise, when our attendants and the luggage had<br/>
come on board, we began our voyage. The Nile itself<br/>
maintains pretty much the same character throughout its<br/>
course. The broad, yellow mass of waters moves slowly<br/>
through the land. Level shores with far-stretching sandbanks, and high broken banks with dark rich soil, and<br/>
provided with pumps and water-wheels, follow each other<br/>
in pretty regular alternation.<br/>
<br/>

distance of the mountains and of the desert. Palm forests<br/>
of almost tropical richness are interspersed with the yellow<br/>
sugar-cane, green bean and waving corn fields.<br/>
<br/>
