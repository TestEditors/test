The tropic of Cancer, the frontier of Nubia, the tropical
air, and by night in the firmament the topmost stars of the
famous Southern Cross, had lured us; they had drawn us
onwards, but we could follow them no longer. Northwards
we must go, and in melancholy mood we scrambled back
to the temple of Isis.
